# About this chronicle

This is the public archive for a Star Wars: Legion Tours of Duty league hosted at Zulu's Board Game Cafe in Bothell, Washington.

Tours of Duty encourages narrative campaign play. Each player fields a Paragon: a named hero who leads their force, appears in every battle, survives the campaign, pursues agendas, and grows through the story.

The chronicle is maintained as a static GitHub Pages site. Entries are curated into this repository rather than submitted through the website.

## Content boundaries

This is a fan-run league chronicle using original player writing and original player-provided images. Do not add official logos, official art, copied lore text, copied rules text, or other copyrighted assets.
