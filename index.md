# Zulu's Tours of Duty Chronicle

Campaign records, paragon dossiers, registers, agenda files, and battle reports from the Star Wars: Legion Tours of Duty league at Zulu's Board Game Cafe in Bothell, Washington.

This site is a curated static archive. Player-written entries are collected out of band, edited into Markdown, and published from this repository.

## Current files

| Section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| [Paragons]({{ '/paragons/' | url }}) | Named heroes who lead each force and anchor each player's narrative. |
| [Registers]({{ '/registers/' | url }}) | Army register records, affiliations, recurring units, and campaign progression notes. |
| [Battle Reports]({{ '/battles/' | url }}) | Narrative summaries, outcomes, images, and cross-links to participants. |
| [Agendas]({{ '/agendas/' | url }}) | Campaign objectives, completed story beats, and unresolved ambitions. |

## Publishing notes

Use original writing and original player-provided images. References to Star Wars names, game terms, and campaign context should support the league chronicle without copying official lore text, logos, art, or rulebook content.
