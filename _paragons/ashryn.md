## Biography

Ashryn Vorexis is a dedicated Jedi Knight General leading the 68th Guardian Corps on a series of peacekeeping missions for the Grand Army of the Republic. Vorexis joined the war effort two years after the Battle of Geonosis and quickly rose to the rank of Knight General after a series of minor victories with her newly trained corps. She is widely known for fairness and a deliberate temperament with a penchant for leaning on diplomatic tactics.

Vorexis spent her early childhood on Ryloth, until her parents saved up enough money to form a spice trading business and eventually moved off-world. During an encounter with Jedi on the Corellian Run, Ashryn discovered she was Force sensitive and was moved to the Jedi Temple to train.

As a youngling and subsequently as a padawan, Ashryn applied herself dutifully, and was not generally known for testing limits or questioning authority. When she was given her first command on a mission to establish contact on a planet in the Outer Rim, she walked directly into a trap. Fortunately, she negotiated an agreement using the savvy skills she learned from her merchant parents and re-engaged with her units before they initiated hostilities to rescue her. Since then, Vorexis has learned to lean more heavily on those under her command for support and protection, as well as to lend her support and protection to them.

On the most recent operation, a mysterious contact reached out to Vorexis, asking her to help secure information vital to the Galactic Senate and Security Council. They have not yet divulged the individuals who sent them, and seem to closely guard those identities. But they gave the impression that this information could help end the war by fueling what they would only term as Order 65. In the meantime, they said, it is imperative to maintain strict confidentiality and keep her clone troopers close.
