## Biography

Raised alongside her twin brother Castor by a retired Republic Admiral, loyalty and a sense
of duty were instilled in Pollyxa at a young age. From the start, she excelled at games of
strategy and cunning, besting even her father by her early teenage years.
Loyal to the Empire that had given her family so much, she enrolled in the Imperial academy
where she excelled in courses on military strategy and operations, quickly catching the eye of
senior naval officers. During her time at the academy, she was the victim of a training accident
involving a dioxis mine that permanently scarred her respiratory system, forcing her to wear a
respirator outside of controlled environments. This did little to dissuade her from her path
however, as she continued to sit at the top of her class.
In recognition of her potential and extreme loyalty, her ascent through the ranks was
accelerated by Imperial Naval Command to her current station. She was given an Arquitens
Class Cruiser and sent to the Outer Rim to project the might of the empire while secretly
completing another objective, the location and collection of artifacts requested by the Grand
Admirals.
Alongside her brother, whom she specifically requested be added to her regiment, she now
roams the Outer Rim, crushing resistance in the pursuit of her objective. All in the hopes that
one day, her competence, a rare commodity for an imperial officer, will allow her to rise in the
ranks of the Imperial Navy and immortalized hers and her brothers' names in history.
