## Biography

Zyx is a Zabrak who was raised on Iridonia, and felt the strain of a such a rigid society, causing him to leave to find his own way in the galaxy. After migrating to Corellia to serve as a welder on capital scale ships, he had a run-in with an arrogant Imperial officer who wanted to criticize Zyx's weld lines. Zyx's reaction was to punch that officer, breaking his jaw. Zyx left the shipyard before the officer came around, fleeing the small life he had made for himself.

Zyx realized he was now wanted by the Empire, so his life took another turn, **the Rebellion**. They would feed him, clothe him, and supply his M-55 repeating blaster with all the blaster gas he needs to exact revenge on the oppressive Empire. He fights mostly because there is no other life for him, and he hopes that others won't endure his fate. He's picked up some combat armor off a dead bounty hunter the Empire sent after him, and that is one of his prized trophies.

His troops began calling him **Big Nuggins** and the name stuck. He's big, he carries a big gun, and there's not a lot more to it than that. His covering fire has saved many of his comrades, as well as drawing enemy fire by being a menace on the battlefield. He knows he may not live to see the change the Rebellion yearns for in the galaxy but he's happy to give his life trying to set everyone free.
