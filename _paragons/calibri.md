## Biography

As the Clone Wars stretched into a grueling third year of open conflict and the Confederacy of Independent Systems began losing their grasp on many Mid Rim systems, General Grievous commissioned the creation of mobile raiding forces to seize Republic supply shipments. These ‘Corsair Fleets’ would consist of small, but well-equipped strike forces operating out of unassuming merchant vessels frequently conducting ship-to-ship boarding actions and raids on planetside supply bases.

The Super Tactical Droid Calibri was constructed on the factory world of Mechis III and designed to command the Mechis Corsair Fleet. While not activated until the closing months of the Clone Wars, Calibri participated in several key operations including a blockade run to the besieged world of Kooriva, and the sabotage of orbital shipyard facilities on Fondor.

Calibri was an obsessive perfectionist that constantly rewrote and bypassed sections of his own programming in order to chase miniscule optimizations. It was because of this tampering that the Mechis Corsair Fleet was unaffected by the shutdown signal sent at the conclusion of the war. Observing the total shutdown of command assets, Calibri immediately returned to Mechis and commandeered the assembly lines to reinforce his army for days on end until Imperial forces finally arrived on planet. The breakout from Mechis III lasted only a few hours with Calibri and a portion of his fleet escaping the system. He now continues operations in the surrounding sectors even into the early Imperial period. His targets have become indiscriminate leading to run-ins with the Empire, clone deserters, resistance movements, and even other pirates. Local spacers tell tales of ghost ships which attack lone vessels before vanishing, leaving no survivors.
